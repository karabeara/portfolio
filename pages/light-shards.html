lightshards

Light shards serve as on-site installations. Painted directly on their respective surfaces, each work serves as a binary clock. Corresponding light waxes and wanes with the years, never falling quite right again.

The collection of light shards below served as part of the CAUSTICS senior exhibition held in February 2019; seven of the ten installed light shards exist to-date.
