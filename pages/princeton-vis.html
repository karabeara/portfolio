princetonvisualarts

Graphic design work for Princeton's Visual Arts (VIS) Department at 185 Nassau Street including a re-branded logo using PRINCETON's geo-typography, a viewbook showcasing graduating seniors' work, and various art exhibition posters.

Worked with Eric Li and Nazlı Ercan to put out the second annual Princeton Visual Arts viewbook. Check out the full layout for Princeton Visual Arts 2019 here.

Designs for circulated posters on Princeton's campus were all rendered and produced in original fonts. Respective posters for The VIS Junior Show, House of VIS, This is not a poster / That's not a book, and Final Runnings Before the After seen below.
