visjuniorshow

Installation of poster series and process work for The VIS Junior Show. The twenty artists in the show were asked individually for the physical place in the world which has influenced their artistic practice, and each individual’s poster was typeset in that respective Topography Typography font. Installed process work included tracings for each location’s full alphabetic set as well as an arm chair from the studio.

This work served in fulfillment of Princeton Visual Arts Department's junior independent work requirement.


2018.
