computergraphicscoursework

Two semesters worth of undergraduate and graduate level computer graphics coursework in COS 426: Computer Graphics and COS 526: Advanced Computer Graphics. Developed functional image editor, mesh editor, raytracer, rasterizer, and particle animator as well as algorithms for Poisson cloning, point cloud registration, and photon mapping.

Advised by Szymon Rusinkiwicz and Amit Bermano.

Python, C++, JavaScript, GLSL. 2017-2019.
