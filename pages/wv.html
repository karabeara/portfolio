west virginia

Field research in West Virginia studying leakage of methane from abandoned oil and natural gas wells.

Independent project involved leading field team in the collection and analysis of oil and gas infrastructure - including locations of pipelines and pipeline junctions - by walking lines and mapping locations of leaks.

Determined number of pipeline leaks per unit length of piping in West Virginia and created a visual map of the organization of gathering pipeline - pipeline connecting wellheads to larger transmission piping.

Published paper expected Fall 2017. Advised by Stuart Riddick and Denise Mauzerall.

ArcGIS, Java. 2016.
