Kara Lynn Bressler is a visual artist, computer scientist, outdoor educator, light observer, and listener. Having grown up in Southwest Florida, she is now based in Los Angeles, CA where she works as a lighting technical director for feature films at DreamWorks Animation. She graduated recently from the Computer Science and Visual Arts departments at Princeton University, having focused on computer graphics and graphic design theory.
